Korean Goral Population Estimation

Research project on estimating the population size of the endangered Korean goral (Naemorhedus caudatus).

  • Learn More

About the Project

This project focuses on population size estimation of the Korean goral using statistical modeling and capture-recapture methods. Our goal is to provide reliable estimates that support conservation and management efforts.
